Types of Motor Vehicles in India

Motorcycles
Two-wheeled vehicles designed for speed and efficiency, popular among commuters and enthusiasts.
Scooters
Lightweight two-wheelers with automatic transmission, ideal for city rides.
Sedans
Comfortable four-door cars offering a balance between the driving person and the passengers
Car
A self-propelled road vehicle designed for passengers, typically with four wheels.
Bus
A large motor vehicle designed to carry a number of passengers between stopping places.
Truck
A heavy-duty vehicle designed for hauling goods and materials.
Van
A versatile vehicle, often used for transportation or cargo, ranging in size from small to large.
Tractor
A strong vehicle, typically used for pulling agricultural equipment.
Fire Engine
A large truck equipped for fire suppression, rescue operations, and related tasks.
Ambulance
A specialized vehicle designed to transport patients to hospitals or medical facilities.
